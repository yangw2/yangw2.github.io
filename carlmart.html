Carlmart App

Carlmart is an app created by Colin James, Khizar Qureshi, Sho Tanaka, and Wesley Yang. Its goal is to reduce waste by enabling people to buy and sell used items.

The app features a search function that helps users quickly find items they need. A well-organized main page displays available items along with their prices, making shopping easy. And

By promoting second-hand purchases, Carlmart supports sustainability and waste reduction.
